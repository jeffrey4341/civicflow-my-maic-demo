# Council Service Charter

**Document type:** Service Charter (SYNTHETIC — demo only)
**Owner:** Office of the Council Secretary
**Reference:** CHT-GEN-000 (fictional)

> A fictional service charter for the CivicFlow MY Mobile demo council
> ("Majlis Demo"). All targets are illustrative.

## Our Commitment to Citizens

The council commits to acknowledging every citizen request, routing it to the
correct department, keeping the citizen informed, and treating personal
information with care. Citizens may submit requests in Bahasa Melayu, English,
Chinese, or Tamil.

## Acknowledgement Targets

- All cases acknowledged on submission with a tracking code.
- Flood-risk and other emergencies: acknowledged within 2 hours.
- Standard cases: acknowledged immediately, first human update within 3 working
  days.

## Resolution Targets by Service

- Drainage (flood-risk): supervisor decision within 4 hours.
- Drainage (routine): inspection within 5 working days.
- Business licensing: complete applications processed within 14 working days.
- Education aid / welfare: officer review within 10 working days.
- Roads, potholes, street lighting, waste: inspection within 7 working days.

## Human Oversight

AI assists officers by detecting language, classifying, retrieving policy, and
drafting replies. **Officers and supervisors make all decisions.** High-risk
actions require supervisor approval. No case is closed by the system alone.

## Languages and Accessibility

Citizens are served in their chosen language. Replies are drafted in the language
the citizen used and reviewed by an officer before sending.

## Feedback and Redress

Citizens may request a review of any decision. This demo does not implement a
real redress workflow; it records the request in the audit timeline.
