# Welfare Education Aid Policy

**Document type:** Policy (SYNTHETIC — demo only)
**Owner:** Community & Welfare Department / Education Support Unit
**Reference:** POL-WEL-003 (fictional)

> Invented welfare policy for the CivicFlow MY Mobile demo. Eligibility figures
> are placeholders and do not reflect any real programme.

## Purpose

This policy governs pre-screening of citizen enquiries about **education aid**
(bantuan pendidikan) and related **welfare** support for school-going children.
Keywords: education aid, bantuan pendidikan, welfare, kebajikan, school, sekolah,
student, biasiswa, bantuan, scholarship, child.

## No Automatic Approval

Education-aid and welfare enquiries are **never auto-approved by the system**.
The AI may pre-screen and assemble a document checklist, but **eligibility is
always decided by a welfare officer** after reviewing supporting documents. The
demo deliberately routes these cases for **officer review** rather than producing
an approval decision.

## Eligibility Factors (Officer-Assessed)

A welfare officer assesses, among other factors: household income band, number of
school-going children, current enrolment, and whether the family already receives
other aid. These are assessed manually — the system does not score eligibility.

## Document Checklist (Pre-Screen)

To prepare an education-aid application, the citizen is asked to gather:

- The child's birth certificate (masked in this demo).
- Proof of school enrolment for the current year.
- Household income evidence (e.g. payslip or income declaration).
- Guardian identity document (NRIC) — masked in this demo.
- Any existing aid reference number, if applicable.

## Information the System Collects

The pre-screen captures the child's school level, the guardian relationship, and
the type of aid requested. It does **not** request bank details or full NRIC in
the demo.

## Service Charter

Officer review of a complete pre-screen is targeted within 10 working days. The
citizen receives a checklist immediately so they can prepare documents.

## Department Routing

Education-aid and welfare enquiries are routed to the **Community & Welfare
Department, Education Support Unit** for officer review.
